Scope of services
A full cost lifecycle, handled in-house

We tailor every engagement — no templated solutions. From the first feasibility study to the settled final account, the financial side of your build stays under control.

01

Feasibility & Pre-Investment

Financial feasibility studies, viability advice, and support for loan applications to financial institutions.

02

Cost Estimate & Planning

Construction cost estimates, advice on alternative designs and materials, and cost planning and control.

03

Tender & Contract Docs

Preparation of tender and contract documents, plus advice on tendering procedures and contractor pre-qualification.

04

Tender Evaluation

Detailed analysis of contractors' tenders with financial and contractual advice on the award of contract.

05

Variations & Cost Control

Evaluation of variations and control of construction costs to keep the final cost within the allocated budget.

06

Interim & Final Account

Periodical valuations and interim payment recommendations, through to settlement of the final account.
